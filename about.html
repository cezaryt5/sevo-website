About SEVO

Simat Elkhair Voluntary Organization - Empowering Sudanese women through community, growth, and hope since 2009.

Our Story

Simat Elkhair Voluntary Organization (SEVO) was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to empower Sudanese women and strengthen communities through sustainable development programs.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationwide organization with over 350 active volunteers and countless success stories across Sudan.

Our Mission

To empower Sudanese women and communities through education, health services, economic opportunities, and sustainable development initiatives that create lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A Sudan where every woman has the opportunity to thrive, every community has access to clean water and healthcare, and sustainable development creates prosperity for all.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, community participation, gender equality, and sustainable development guide everything we do at SEVO.

Our Journey

2009

The Beginning

SEVO was founded by a group of passionate volunteers dedicated to helping women in rural Sudan.

2012

First Water Project

Completed our first clean water well project, providing safe water to over 500 families.

2015

Skills Training Center

Opened our first vocational training center, empowering women with marketable skills.

2018

Health Outreach

Launched mobile health clinics reaching remote communities across three states.

2022

National Expansion

Expanded operations to all regions of Sudan, serving over 50 communities.
