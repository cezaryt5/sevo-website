Our Impact

Measurable results. Real communities. Lasting change.

Impact Overview

Since 2009, SEVO has touched thousands of lives across Sudan through our comprehensive programs in water access, women's empowerment, health, and education.

Clean Water Access

Our well projects have provided clean water to over 15,000 people in rural communities, reducing waterborne diseases and saving women hours of daily water collection.

Women's Empowerment

Over 1,200 women have completed our vocational training programs, with 70% starting their own businesses or securing employment within a year.

Health Outreach

Our mobile clinics have provided over 50,000 medical consultations and vaccinations in remote communities without access to healthcare facilities.

Education Support

We've supported 500+ girls to continue their education through scholarships, school supplies, and community learning centers.
