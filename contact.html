Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

90% of donations go directly to our programs. 10% covers administrative costs. Detailed breakdowns are in our annual reports.

Can I donate in other currencies?

Yes, we accept donations in multiple currencies including SDG, EUR, and GBP. Contact us for details.

How do I volunteer?

Visit our contact page and select 'Volunteer' as the subject. We'll get back to you with opportunities.
